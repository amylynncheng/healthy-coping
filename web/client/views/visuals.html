Analyzing the Depression Screening results:

  • Each colored bar represents a question on the PHQ-9 screening survey.
  • The length of each bar is equivalent the numerical value of the patient's answer
    (very often = 3, often = 2, sometimes = 1, never = 0)
  • The total length of one entire bar is equal to the summed score of all indiviual questions.
  • The severity of the patient's depression is notated by the colored region that the total score falls into.
