For Patients

The app will help you self-manage the complexities that come with diabetes. It is equipped with features such as: monitoring blood glucose levels, physical activity, and weight; providing an instant screening service for emotional coping with real-time results; and food recommendations catered to helping diabetics eat healthy.

For Physicians

When your diabetic patients use our self-management app, you can monitor their status without scheduling in-person appointments. View their results on this website with the click of a button or even modify the features to better fit your patients.
